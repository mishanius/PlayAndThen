# Privacy Policy for PlayAndThen

**Last updated: January 23, 2026**

## Overview

PlayAndThen is a parental control app that helps manage screen time on YouTube Kids by requiring children to complete educational games before watching videos.

## Data Collection

**PlayAndThen does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What the app accesses (locally only):

- **YouTube Kids app activity**: The app monitors when YouTube Kids is opened to trigger educational games. This data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

- **Accessibility Service**: PlayAndThen uses Android's Accessibility Service solely to detect YouTube Kids usage and display educational games. No data from this service is collected, stored, or shared.

### What the app does NOT do:

- Does not collect personal information
- Does not track browsing history
- Does not record video watching habits
- Does not transmit any data to external servers
- Does not use analytics or tracking services
- Does not contain advertisements
- Does not make network requests

## Data Storage

All app settings (such as game preferences) are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app
- Is not accessible to other apps

## Children's Privacy

PlayAndThen is designed for use by families with children. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:
- We do not collect any data from children
- We do not require account creation
- We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

## Accessibility Service Usage

This app requires the Accessibility Service permission to function. This permission is used exclusively to:
1. Detect when YouTube Kids app is opened
2. Display educational games as an overlay
3. Allow interaction with the game overlay

The Accessibility Service does NOT:
- Read or store any content from other apps
- Capture screenshots or screen recordings
- Access passwords, messages, or personal data
- Transmit any information externally

## Third-Party Services

PlayAndThen does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.
