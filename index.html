Accessibility Service Disclosure

PlayAndThen uses Android's Accessibility Service to enhance your child's educational experience during screen time.

What We Access

  • YouTube and YouTube Kids app screens — to detect when your child is watching videos

What We Do With It

  • Detect video playback — so we know when to show educational games
  • Detect video language — to enforce your language preferences
  • Show game overlays — educational mini-games appear on top of the video player

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data
  • We do NOT access other apps besides YouTube/YouTube Kids
  • We do NOT record screen content or keystrokes
  • We do NOT access passwords, messages, or sensitive information
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties

Data Safety

  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • No accessibility data leaves your device
  • No user data is collected or transmitted
